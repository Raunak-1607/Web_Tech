India Reveal Cash Reward for Champions Trophy Winners

March 20, 2025

The Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) announced a cash reward of ₹58 crore for the Indian cricket team following their triumphant campaign in the ICC Champions Trophy 2025. The reward recognizes the outstanding performances of the players, coaching staff, support personnel, and members of the selection committee. India remained unbeaten throughout the tournament and defeated New Zealand in the final to secure their third Champions Trophy title. Fans across the country celebrated the historic achievement, praising the team's consistency and determination. BCCI officials described the victory as a proud moment for Indian cricket and emphasized the importance of rewarding excellence and dedication.
