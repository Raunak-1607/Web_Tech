AIUB Students Win National Programming Contest

July 2, 2026 | By Admin | Education

A team of talented students from American International University-Bangladesh (AIUB) secured first place in the National Programming Contest 2025, held at Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET). The competition featured more than 150 teams from universities across the country.

The AIUB team demonstrated outstanding problem-solving abilities, teamwork, and programming skills throughout the contest. They successfully solved several challenging algorithmic problems within the allotted time, outperforming many experienced competitors.

The victory highlights AIUB's strong emphasis on computer science education and competitive programming. The university continues to encourage students to participate in national and international competitions, helping them develop practical skills and gain valuable experience.
